About SPAx '26

Where ambition meets
professional clarity.

The Student Professional Awareness Conference (SPAx '26) is IEEE UNIBEN's flagship career-development event — designed to equip engineering and technology students with the mindset, networks, and practical insights needed to thrive beyond graduation.

Through expert-led sessions on industry trends, professional ethics, emerging technologies, and career strategy, SPAx transforms academic knowledge into actionable professional advantage.

Whether you're exploring your first internship or preparing for graduate roles, SPAx '26 gives you the tools to navigate your path with confidence.
